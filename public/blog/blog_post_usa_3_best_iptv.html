IPTV Legality in USA

IPTV itself is legal. What matters is the content source:

  • ✅ Legal: Licensed streaming services distributing content they have rights to
  • ❌ Illegal: Unauthorized streaming of paid content (movies, sports, HBO, etc.)

Most reputable providers (like Smartplay) have legal licensing agreements. Always verify your provider's legitimacy.
